Yorkshire Pudding

Ingredients

  • 140g plain flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • Sunflower oil (for cooking)

Method

  1. Heat oven to 230C/fan 210C/gas 8.
  2. Drizzle sunflower oil into tins and heat in oven.
  3. Mix flour and eggs until smooth.
  4. Gradually add milk and beat until lump-free. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour batter into tins and bake 20–25 mins until puffed and browned.
  6. Serve immediately or freeze for up to 1 month.

Pastry

Ingredients

  • 175g/6oz plain flour
  • Pinch salt
  • 85g/3oz butter, cubed
  • 2–3 tbsp cold water

Method

  1. Put flour and salt in a large bowl and add the butter cubes.
  2. Rub butter into flour with fingertips until it looks like coarse breadcrumbs.
  3. Stir in 1–2 tbsp water with a knife, adding more only if needed, until dough binds.
  4. Gently knead until smooth — handle as little as possible.
  5. Alternatively, use a food processor to combine flour, salt, and butter, then add water gradually.
  6. Wrap in cling film and chill for 30 mins before use, or roll out and rest before baking.
